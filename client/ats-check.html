Quick ATS Optimization Tips

Use Standard Section Headers

Use conventional headers like "Experience", "Education", "Skills" instead of creative alternatives.

Include Relevant Keywords

Match keywords from the job description naturally throughout your resume.

Use Simple Formatting

Avoid complex layouts, graphics, and unusual fonts that ATS can't parse.

Quantify Achievements

Include numbers, percentages, and metrics to demonstrate impact.
